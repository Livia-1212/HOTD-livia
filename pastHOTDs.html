What this is about?

This app is an inspiration from a book called Atomic Habits by James Clear. It is a simple way to keep track of your day and motivate you to do good things matter in your life. You can add your best highlights of your day and can share among your friends and family by pressing HOTD!.


This app complete privacy and storage friendly. You can add it to your desktop and use it like a native app. You can see this article for help.
